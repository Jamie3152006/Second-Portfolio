Optical Illusion

I had a hard time figuring out how I would make an optical illusion, but it ended up being a pretty fun and experimental process for me. I just started playing around with blendmodes and angles, which really affected the movement of my shapes and how they cross each other, creating cool effects. The look I was going for was a flower pattern that can be seen with in traditional porcelain China, giving it a sense of familarity and cultural grounding.

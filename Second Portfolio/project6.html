Data Portrait

The final p5js sketch created reflects personal data taken from my day to day for a week. The data was how many times did I put my earbuds in throughout the week. I wanted to visualize the contrast between the outside world and the inner one within our minds, and how for me personally, serves as a retreat from the chaos of the outside world. The movement within the sketch is affected by my data, creating noise patterns that reflect my concept.

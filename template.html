Interactive Typography

Prototypes - JavaScript Sketches

This project in experimenting on how to make typography and type more interesting and have user interactivity, offering a new way of iterating and experiencing design. The challenge was to not only create movement or a function that was fitting of the chosen word, but to also make it look designed and interesting, almost like a poster. After prototyping in nine different ways, these were the three I settled on as I though the concepts were the most developed and cohesive.

Colors of the World

An exploratory project in reconsidering the world around us. For a few weeks, pictures were taken around the surrounding environment that is New York City, and the colors from the pictures were digitally abstracted to show color combinations. Inspired by "A Dictionary of Color Combinations" by Wada Sanzo, this projects takes a closer look at what we see in our daily lives and turns it into a more whimsical output, showing artistry in simplicity.

Impact of LA Fires

A distilled news article from the New York Times Newspaper turned into visuals drawn digitally to illustrate the seriousness of the wildfires that occured in the area of Los Angeles California. Experimenting with ways to reconsider the way someone can consume media even when the topic is more serious, demonstrating illustration and advertising strategy.

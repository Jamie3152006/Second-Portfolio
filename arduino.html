The Comfort

A project that uses the irony of useless machines. Flips the narrative of human reliance on machines; this jacket, meant to provide comfort and grounding in a time of anxiety or panic, fails to do so, showing the space in which machines are not the sole solution to human problems nor do they understand emotions the same way. Masks itself as a "health machine" but shows the non-linear healing process of human truama.

Bird Box

A practice in physical computing. The box was made as a personal momento to childhood stories and experiecnes, such as bird motifs, childhood songs, and a cultural style. The box acts like a jack-in-a-box, where the bird pops out once the sensor on the side detects a hand, and musics starts playing. Fun, playful, and personal.

What's Inside?

This website contains designs that were made from p5js, a coding platform that provided the basis for most of my first JavaScript sketches. As I move into the Spring semester of 2026, there is a transition to Visual Studio Code and other platforms and mediums for my creative work.
These designs were created as introduction to creative coding and learning how to incorporate design and storytelling skills with coding languages such as JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. This is my introduction to the world of coding and how I can take abstract prompts and create something meaningful and personal.
Within this portfolio website, it contains the projects that were created within my Sophomore year at Parsons the School of Design, studying for a BFA in Design and Technology.
There is also a selection of my work outside DT (Design and Technology) to allow viewing of the wide range of styles and mediums I have created in, showcasing my skill set.
